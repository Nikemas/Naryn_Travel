Naryn is one of the cities of Kyrgyzstan. It is located 186 km from the border with China, at the intersection of the Bishkek-Torugart highway. Naryn is the administrative center of the Naryn region. Museums, institutes, various buildings of administrative and state importance are located here. Like many other Central Asian cities, Naryn emerged as a small fortification on the trade routes that led from East Turkestan (Kashgar) to Central Asia. Until the 19th century, there was no city as such in this area.
